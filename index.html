PreciseFlow introduces a novel approach to stabilize editing signals in flow-based video editing models. Our method achieves precise temporal consistency and high-quality editing results while maintaining the original video's motion dynamics.

Overview

Flow-based video editing models have shown promising results, but maintaining temporal consistency while achieving precise edits remains challenging. PreciseFlow addresses this by stabilizing the editing signal throughout the flow-based generation process.
